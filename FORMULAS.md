# All the formulas used in this project

---

**Distance between two bodies in 3D:**
$$r = \sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2 + (y_2-y_1)^2 + (z_2-z_1)^2}$$

**Gravitational force magnitude:**
$$F = G \frac{m_1 m_2}{r^2}$$

**Force direction (unit vector):**
$$\hat{r} = \frac{dx}{r}, \frac{dy}{r}, \frac{dz}{r}$$

**Acceleration from force:**
$$a = \frac{F}{m}$$

**Velocity update:**
$$v = v + a \cdot dt$$

**Position update:**
$$x = x + v \cdot dt$$
